About


Hello! I am Software Engineer from San Diego, CA who recently picked up and moved to Sydney, Australia on a Work Holiday Visa. It was a huge step for me to leave everything I know and move halfway around the world. However, I am extremely excited for this new adventure, and to meet many incredible people along the way. I'm not really sure what to expect this next year, but I would love to land a position that allows me to explore this beautiful city and country for more than just a year. I know the tech industry in Sydney is growing rapidly, and I would love to be able to contribute to its growth.

Now a little about myself. I played four years of professional baseball as an infielder in the Miami Marlins organization prior to beginning college. Not only was it a great experience, but I learned many valuable life lessons along the way. Through baseball I learned that nothing comes easy, and that having a strong work ethic is essential for success. This mentality has carried over into my everyday life, making me strive to be the best at whatever I do.

While I understand the value of being driven, I also know how important it is to find a balance between work and fun. In my spare time, I enjoy working out, biking, backpacking, golfing, building things, doing art projects, enjoying the great craft beer in San Diego, going to music events and festivals, and mimosa Sundays.
