About


Hello! I am a recent graduate of San Diego State University in Computer Science. I have always been fascinated with technology and how it drives the world around us. Now more than ever, we are able to use technology to push our innovative boundaries, and I am excited to contribute to this growing field. Recently, I have started getting into web development, with more emphasis on front-end development.

Prior to beginning college, I played four years of professional baseball as an infielder in the Miami Marlins organization. Not only was it a great experience, but I learned many valuable life lessons along the way. Through baseball I learned that nothing comes easy, and that having a strong work ethic is essential for success. This mentality has carried over into my everyday life, making me strive to be the best at whatever I do.

While I understand the value of being driven, I also know how important it is to find a balance between work and fun. When I have free time, I enjoy working out, biking, backpacking, golfing, enjoying the great craft beer in San Diego, and various art projects.

Projects

Raspberry Pi Smart Room

I am currently working on this project. My goal for this project is to make an electronic, wall-mounted calendar and notification center using an old laptop screen. I am currently able to synchronize my Google calendar and other notifications, such as the weather, using a web server and my Raspberry Pi. In addition to this, I would also like to add voice activation capabilities to control the lights in my room or other various tasks. If I am able to implement this, I may add motion detection functionality to the system to conserve energy when nobody is present in the room.
