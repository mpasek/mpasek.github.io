While I understand the value of being driven, I also know how important it is to find a balance between work and fun. In my spare time, I enjoy working out, biking, backpacking, golfing, building things, doing art projects, enjoying the great craft beer in San Diego, going to music events and festivals, and mimosa Sundays.
